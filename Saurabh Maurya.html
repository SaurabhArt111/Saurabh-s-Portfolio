The Artist

Saurabh Art 111
"Art is not what you see, but what you make others see."

Hi, I'm Saurabh — an artist passionate about blending traditional and digital styles. My journey started with pencil sketches and grew into anime-inspired art, portraits, and creative designs.

From bold pen art to detailed pencil work, every piece reflects both technique and emotion. I draw inspiration from real personalities and fictional worlds, capturing their essence on paper and screen.
